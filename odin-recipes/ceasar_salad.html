Why should you choose a Salad

Salads often combine vegetables, fruits, nuts, seeds, and lean proteins, giving you a high concentration of vitamins (A, C, K, folate), minerals (potassium, magnesium), and antioxidants without excessive calories. Many salad ingredients (lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes) have high water content, helping with hydration. Also, leafy greens are rich in dietary fiber, which supports healthy digestion, regulates blood sugar. All of which keeps you feeling full longer — helpful for weight control

First you want to ensure that you'll have the best dressing for the salad. This is what makes or breaks a salad, In its most basic form, a salad is simply lettuce (Romaine/Spinach/Kale etc). However, the quality of ingredients in which you'll pair with the lettuce is what makes the salad so appetizing.

Ingredients

  • 3 Anchovy Fillets
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic (Finely-Diced)
  • ½ Lemon (Juiced)
  • 2 Tablespoons of Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 Large Egg Yolk
  • 1 Tablesppon of Dijon Mustard
  • A Splash of Worcestershire Sauce
  • ¼ Cup of Olive Oil
  • ½ head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons croutons

& Lastly add Salt/Pepper to your level of satisfaction

Creating the Dressing

  1. Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl
  2. Add Lemon Juice, Red Wine Vinegar, Dijon Mustard, Egg Yolk, and Worcestershire sauce;
  3. Whisk until Smooth and Creamy, but Gradually stream in Olive Oil while whisking constantly
  4. Lastly, Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated.
